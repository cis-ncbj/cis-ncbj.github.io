Świerk Computing Centre.

Świerk Computing Centre (CIŚ) mission is to deliver high-quality modern IT services to all entities involved in development of nuclear power industry sector in Poland, as well as to Polish government agencies and research institutions. We operate one of the largest super computers in Poland
